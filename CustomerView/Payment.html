STEPS DESCRIPTION
Step 1 Log-in to your Gcash Account.
Step 2 Select PAY BILLS. Under the CABLE/INTERNET category, select SOUTH LUZON CABLE TV.
Step 3 Input your Name and 13-digit Account Number/Reference Number.
Step 4 Input the amount you have to pay.
Step 5 Click Next to PROCEED to CONFIRM transaction.
Step 6 Take a SCREENSHOT of the successful payment transaction for proof of payment.
